Junkyard Drive

Full throttle scandi garage rock

Since their inception, Junkyard Drive has made an indelible mark on the rock scene with their intense energy and unwavering passion. Having solidified their reputation with the acclaimed albums Black Coffee and Electric Love, they have showcased their remarkable ability to evolve and create music that resonates deeply.

Their latest album, Look at Me Now, signifies not just a musical rebirth for Junkyard Drive, but also a profoundly personal journey. This collection invites listeners into the struggles and triumphs of the band members, exploring themes such as gender identity, addiction, and mental healthâ€”issues that lie at the core of their experiences. With raw honesty, Junkyard Drive crafts narratives that challenge and enrich, underscoring their commitment to creating music that makes a meaningful impact.

As they continue to captivate their fans with heartfelt stories and powerful melodies, Junkyard Drive remains true to their roots while daring to explore new musical terrains. They are eager to elevate their artistry to new heights and redefine what modern rock music can be. Holding fast to their title as one of Denmark's premier rock bands, Junkyard Drive looks forward to fresh adventures on the rock stage.
